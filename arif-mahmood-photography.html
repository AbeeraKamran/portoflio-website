ARIF MAHMOOD

  • Web Design

Arif Mahmood is the most prolific and versatile photojournalist in Pakistan. His body of work is extensive, with a particular and long-standing interest in the shrine of Lal Shahbaz Qalander in Sehwan. He is also heads Paksitan's only home-grown photojournalism agency, White Star (Dawn News group).

The brief was to build an elegant and non intrusive website, where nothing took attention away from the photographs. The wesbite serves both as a portoflio for Mahmood and a digital archive, featuring both his published and unpublished work.
