ABOUT

  • Nefarious internetting

I'm Abeera, a graphic designer and front-end web developer*. I work with all kinds of amazing people, from small business owners to artist collectives, across both print and digital media.

Every project I take on is a collaborative experience with my clients because working with people that inspire me and ideas that move me is extremeley important to my design practice. I want to build meaningful work. Work that is not preoccupied with being beautiful, but provocative, questioning and ultimately impossible to ignore.

If you would like to work with me or just want to say hello you can email me here. All the code for this website is available on GitHub.

*shout out to all the terrible developers I worked with along the way, their awful work forcing me into the wonderful world of web dev and now I only wish I'd learned this magic sooner.
