ANT HILL

  • Typography

This project came as a result of an identity design I was working on for an exhbition. At the time it was limited to the title design. After the exhbition I continued playing with it, enjoying building it and deconstucting it, as an exercise in understanding letterforms.

Each letter is built from a grid of diamond shapes, each diamond echoing city lights, an analogy for the terrifying and thrilling insomnia Karachi is engulfed in.

The typeface is nearly complete and I will be uploading it in it's entirety very soon.
